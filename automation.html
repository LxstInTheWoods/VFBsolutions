What is Automation & Orchestration?

Automation and orchestration refer to the use of technology to perform tasks and processes with minimal human intervention. Automation handles repetitive tasks, while orchestration coordinates multiple automated processes to work together seamlessly, ensuring optimal workflow across your IT environment.
